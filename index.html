About Me

Hello! I'm Alex Ross, an illustrator and costume designer with a love for storytelling and worldbuilding. Currently, I am a Sophomore at Boise State University majoring in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile (GIMM) with a minor in Anthropology. I have experience writing code in C#, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS and have worked on both game and web development projects. I am also in the Work Study program with the university's Sculpture department where I focus on woodworking, metalsmithing, and textile manipulation.

Active Projects

Sakizo Apple Pie Cosplay: Popular fashion illustrator Sakizo's 2023 Apple Pie design is being brought to life, including an entirely handmade parasol.

Factory Reset: A 2.5D horror RPG set in an abandoned factory. This project is for my Senior year Individual Game Project!

Blue Hour: My personal webcomic set in a world I have been working on for a very long time. Finally these ideas are being brought to the "big" screen (aka Webtoon).
